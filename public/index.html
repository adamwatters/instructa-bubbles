instructaBUBBLES

What is this?

A navigable bubble chart of 500 recent instructables from Instructables.com. Bubbles are sized by number of views.

How do I navigate this so called "navigable bubble chart"?

  • double click on a bubble to open it

  • double click outside of a bubble to return to the Categories level

  • when you reach a single InstructaBubble, double click to open the Instructable in a new tab

  • two-finger swipe to zoom in/out (with cursor over a bubble)

  • click-and-drag to pan (ditto last paren)
